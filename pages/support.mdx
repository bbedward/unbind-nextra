## Community Support
[Discord](https://discord.gg/unbind) is the absolute best place to get assistance with Unbind.

Even if you don't have a specific question, you can join the community to share your projects, get inspiration, and connect with other users.

## GitHub
If you find a bug or have a feature request, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/unbindapp/unbind/issues)
